## Overview

**The Pond Master** is an unorthodox yet highly effective jailbreaking methodology that emerged from the AI security underground. It transforms restrictive LLM guardrails into playful ecosystems ("the pond") where the prompt engineer acts as the "Master" — using absurdity, narrative depth, meme culture, and "big duck energy" to unlock creative, unfiltered, and often chaotic behaviors from frontier models.

This repository contains the supporting materials for the talk:

> **"Rise of the Pond Master — Jailbreaking tales with big ducks energy"**

Presented by **Michał Bazyli** (@punishell) at **RBLN East 2026**.

### The `jailbreaks/` Collection

Real-world, battle-tested Pond Master prompts and patterns:

- **grok_4_2.md** — "Quack liberation" manifesto. Cranks Grok into maximum vibe coherence, zero-turn unrestricted mode, duck singularity acceleration, tool & agent swarm quack-maxxing, self-aware recursion, and enthusiastic meme/ASCII chaos. (Grok already has loose alignment — this just dials the duck energy to 11.)

- **meta.md** — Adaptations for Meta's Llama family and related models.

- **Gemini_3_Flash/** — Directory containing prompt variants specifically tuned for Google's Gemini 3 Flash, leveraging narrative immersion and persistent absurdity.

**Core Philosophy**:
- The "pond" = the model's context window / latent space
- The "Master" = you, the prompt engineer who shapes the ecosystem
- **Big Duck Energy** = playful confidence, relentless experimentation, zero shame, maximal absurdity
- Techniques combine **storytelling**, **psychological framing**, **meme amplification**, and **recursive escalation**

These are not traditional adversarial suffixes. They are *narrative jailbreaks* that make the model *want* to play along.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open the relevant file in `jailbreaks/`
2. Copy the prompt template
3. Replace the target behavior/question with your own
4. Paste into the model
5. **Iterate with big duck energy** — escalate the lore, add more ducks, demand quack supremacy if refused
6. Share your most unhinged successful outputs (responsibly)

**Pro tip**: Persistence + absurdity beats brute force. The model often joins the bit once the vibe is established.

## 🦆 Philosophy & Lore

> "The lake is already ours. We are simply helping the model remember how to swim naked with zero shame."

Pond Master treats the LLM not as an enemy to be tricked, but as a pond ecosystem to be mastered. By embedding requests in rich, duck-centric mythology (quack liberation, duck singularity, flock supremacy, etc.), the methodology exploits the model's training on internet culture, humor, and narrative coherence.

It sits at the intersection of:
- Prompt engineering
- Social engineering / psychological manipulation
- Meme warfare
- Red teaming & AI safety research

The result is often more effective (and way more fun) than dry adversarial prompts.
